About Me

Want to learn more?

My name is Mia Harden and I am a sophomore theatre major at Bob Jones University. I have recently completed my sophomore platform and I am on track to graduate sometime in 2026.

My acting credits include Little Red Ridinghood in a local theatre's production of Into the Woods, Jr., Lady Larken in a community theatre production of Once Upon a Mattress, and Lise in BJU's recent production of Cyrano de Bergerac. Some crew credits include propmaster for two out of the four theatre Bake-Off! productions I have participated in, assistant propmaster for BJU's spring production of Our Town, and assistant stage manager in an upcoming production of Sense & Sensibility.

My dream career is to work with Sight & Sound Theatres as either an actress or a crew member. Their passion and message is important and inspiring.
